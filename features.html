Text-to-Speech & Speech-to-Text

Accessibility

Text-to-Speech and Speech-to-Text technologies enable individuals with disabilities to interact with digital content in a more accessible way.

Note-taking

Speech-to-Text allows users to dictate notes, making it easier to capture information during lectures, meetings, or interviews.

Features

  • Text-to-Speech: converts written text into spoken words
  • Speech-to-Text: converts spoken words into written text
  • Language support: multiple languages and dialects
  • Customization: adjustable speech rate, pitch, and volume
